About Us

Why choose SRI NRI Services

Your Trusted Partner in India

SRI NRI Services was established with a simple mission: to provide reliable, comprehensive assistance to Non-Resident Indians who need support managing affairs back home.

We understand the challenges of managing properties, coordinating services, and caring for loved ones from afar. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing peace of mind through our reliable and personalized services.

With years of experience and a network of trusted partners across India, we ensure that your needs are met with the highest standards of quality and professionalism.
